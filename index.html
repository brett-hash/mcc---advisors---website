For Employers

Our Search Process

From initial consultation to successful integration, we guide you through every step with strategic insight and market intelligence.

01

Discovery & Strategy

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your firm's culture, strategic goals, and the specific requirements for the role.

  • Confidential needs assessment
  • Competitive market analysis
  • Position specification development
  • Search strategy alignment
02

Targeted Candidate Identification

We leverage our network to identify candidates who match both technical requirements and cultural fit.

  • Direct outreach to qualified candidates
  • Network referrals and recommendations
  • Confidential market mapping
  • Preliminary candidate screening
03

Rigorous Vetting & Assessment

Every candidate undergoes comprehensive evaluation. We present only candidates we would hire ourselves.

  • In-depth candidate interviews
  • Practice capability assessment
  • Reference and credential verification
  • Detailed candidate presentations
04

Interview Coordination & Negotiation

We manage the entire interview process and facilitate negotiations to reach mutually beneficial terms.

  • Interview scheduling and logistics
  • Candidate preparation and coaching
  • Offer negotiation and structuring
  • Resignation and transition guidance
05

Integration & Follow-Up

Our commitment doesn't end at acceptance. We provide 90-day integration support.

  • Start date coordination
  • 30/60/90-day check-ins
  • Integration support and troubleshooting
  • 90-day placement guarantee

Your Career Journey With Us

We help you strategically advance your career with opportunities that align with your long-term goals.

01

Confidential Initial Consultation

We start with a no-obligation conversation to understand your goals. Everything remains completely confidential.

  • Career goals assessment
  • Practice area evaluation
  • Market positioning analysis
  • Compensation benchmarking
02

Strategic Opportunity Matching

We present only opportunities that genuinely align with your goals. We never share your information without explicit authorization.

  • Curated opportunity presentation
  • Detailed firm and role briefings
  • Culture and compensation insights
  • Strategic timing recommendations
03

Interview Preparation & Advocacy

We prepare you thoroughly and advocate on your behalf throughout the process.

  • Interview strategy and preparation
  • Firm culture and interviewer insights
  • Compensation negotiation support
  • Real-time feedback and adjustments
04

Offer Negotiation

We ensure you receive competitive terms that reflect your value in the market.

  • Offer review and analysis
  • Market benchmarking
  • Negotiation strategy
  • Transition planning
05

Long-Term Career Partnership

Your relationship with us doesn't end when you accept an offer. We remain a resource throughout your career.

  • Integration support
  • Ongoing career counsel
  • Future opportunity alerts
  • Market intelligence updates
